About Me

Spencer is a student at the University of Texas at Austin but is originally from Southlake, Texas. In high school, he was named 2nd chair all-state pianist in the state of Texas, he was selected to perform with the Carroll Jazz Orchestra at the 23rd annual Essentially Ellington national competition at Lincoln Center in New York City, and he was awarded 5th prize in the Texas state PASIC keyboard percussion solo competition.

Spencer has been teaching private music lessons for over 4 years with dozens of satisfied students, and he has been fortunate enough to study with musicians including Stefan Karlsson, Jeff Hellmer, Sergio Pamies, Stockton Helbing, David Lown, and others.
